GLIMPSE OF TECHFEST 2022

TECH FEST  2022

TechFest - 2K22 As the name suggests it is the Technical Event of PCET'S PIMPRI CHINCHWAD COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING. The event is organised by the First Year Department of APPLIED SCIENCE AND HUMANITIES. The Event has total 9 sub-events and various teams giving the opportunities in various branches of engineering, to each and every First Year Student to showcase their talents and skills.
